🚀 GigShield AI+

AI-Powered Income Protection for Gig Workers

📌 Problem

Gig delivery workers face income loss due to disruptions like heavy rain, heat, pollution, and curfews. These situations can cause up to 20–30% income loss, with no reliable protection system available.

💡 Our Solution

GigShield AI+ is a hybrid parametric insurance platform that detects disruptions, verifies income loss, and provides instant payouts automatically — ensuring fairness and preventing fraud.

📊 System Workflow

🛡️ Smart Fraud Protection

Our system prevents GPS spoofing and fraud using movement analysis, activity validation, and AI-based risk scoring to ensure only genuine claims are approved.
